She CAn

She CAn is a non-profit company dedicated to empowering young women from the Cape flats and townships through career guidance, feminine wellness, and self-empowerment initiatives.

Contact us

Our Strategic Direction

Clear mission and vision guiding our commitment to empowering young women

M

Our Mission

To inspire confidence in young women from underserved communities to drive social upliftment by providing access to career guidance, wellness education, and self-empowerment tools.

V

Our Vision

To see every young woman from underserved communities confidently pursue her potential, empowered with knowledge, wellness, and purpose.

She CAn Pillars

Our three foundational pillars that guide everything we do to empower young women from underserved communities

01

Career Guidance

We equip academically high-performing high school girls from underserved communities with the knowledge and tools to navigate their future careers.

02

Feminine Wellness

We promote dignity and wellbeing by providing education on menstrual health, hygiene practices, mental wellness and personal care.

03

Self-empowerment and Confidence Building

We create safe and inspiring spaces where young women can find their voice, build self-belief, build their brand and embrace their unique value.
